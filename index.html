    New Product Launch - Skincare Serum

    Create a promotional video showcasing the benefits of a new skincare serum. The video should start by highlighting the key ingredients and their benefits, followed by photos of the serum being applied to the skin. Include before-and-after comparisons showing visible improvements in skin texture and brightness. Emphasize the key messages: "Reveal Your Radiance," "Clinically Proven Ingredients," and "Glowing Skin in Just 7 Days."

    Limited-Time Offer - Fitness App Subscription

    Generate a promotional video to advertise a limited-time offer for a fitness app subscription. The video should showcase photos of diverse workout routines, highlighting features like personalized plans and progress tracking. Include key messages such as "Transform Your Workout," "Join Now for 50% Off," and "Your Fitness Journey Starts Here."
